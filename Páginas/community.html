Meet our Ohana!

Buenos dias, Matosinhos!

Come surf with us in a mix of urban cityscape and nature. The ocean breeze winds and the sound of the boardwalk buzzing around us, it's a unique hub for surfers of all ages and experience levels. We're focused on creating a safe and inclusive space for everyone, while bringing the surfing community together. We are as welcoming and warm as the Northern Portugal weather.

Don't be shy, come surf with us!

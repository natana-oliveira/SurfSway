About Us

Who we are

We are a women's surf society based in Portugal, fostering an incredible community that welcomes women of all ages and skill levels who share a passion for surfing and the ocean.

Surf Sway was born out of the need to build a sense of community. We knew we had to share what we felt with other women - the feeling of empowerment, encouragement and friendship through surfing. We truly believe that through our individual stories and community strength, we can inspire people to face insecurities and come together through the simple act of trying something new. Together we are paving the way for a more welcoming environment at sea, where you don't have to fit a certain mold to be accepted.

Why do we exist

We're here to inspire women from all walks of life by embracing our differences both in and out of the water. We invite everyone to come surf, paddle, watch or just laugh with us. No matter your age or skill level, we want everyone to come along! We want to break down the barrier of intimidation that keeps you from getting into the water. Whether you're just starting out or have been surfing for a while. The supportive and encouraging atmosphere within our group ensures you feel welcome in our surfing community.

Our Vision

"We see a world where surfing is a tool for overcoming barriers and unifying people. Our vision is to create a support network that offers welcoming spaces for women of all backgrounds. By uniting through a shared love for surfing, we aspire to bring joy and empowerment to our members while making a positive impact in our communities."
